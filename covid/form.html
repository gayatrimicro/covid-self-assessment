Because there is currently no vaccine to prevent infection, the best way to protect yourself is to avoid being exposed to this virus. The CDC recommends the following additional steps:

1. Wash your hands often with soap and water for at least 20 seconds, especially after blowing your nose, coughing, or sneezing; going to the bathroom; and before eating or preparing food.

2. Use an alcohol-based hand sanitizer that contains at least 60 percent alcohol if soap and water are not available.

3. Avoid touching your eyes, nose and mouth with unwashed hands.

4. Avoid close contact with people who are sick.

5. Stay home when you are sick.

6. Cover your cough or sneeze with a tissue, then throw the tissue in the trash.

7. Clean and disinfect frequently touched objects and surfaces.
